A Story
I Didn't Plan

by someone who just…
couldn't ignore it anymore

✦ ✦ ✦
Chapter 1 The First Chapter

I don't remember the exact moment it started.

There wasn't some dramatic scene or perfect timing.

But somewhere between normal days and random conversations…
you became someone I started noticing a little more than everyone else.

At first, it was nothing serious.
Just another person… just another conversation.

Or at least, that's what I told myself.

1
Chapter 2 The Unexpected Plot Twist

Slowly, things changed.

It wasn't something big —
just small moments… random thoughts…
finding reasons to check my phone a bit more often.

And without even realizing it,
you became part of my daily routine.

Not in a loud way.
Not in a dramatic way.

Just quietly… but constantly.

2
Chapter 3 The Chaos You Created

I won't lie… you made things a little chaotic for me.

Suddenly, I was overthinking simple things.
Smiling at random messages.
Replaying conversations for no reason.

And yeah… sometimes messing things up too.

But one thing was always real —
how I felt.

3
Chapter 4 Things I Never Said

There were a lot of things I never said properly.

Not because I didn't feel them…
but because I didn't know how to.

And maybe when I finally tried…
it didn't come out the way it should have.

If I made things uncomfortable or confusing,
I'm genuinely sorry.

That was never my intention.

I just… didn't want to hide it anymore.

4
Chapter 5 If This Was a Story

If this was just a story,
I'd probably write it in a simple way.

Two people…
figuring things out slowly,
without pressure, without expectations.

Talking normally.
Laughing at small things.
Being there for each other.

Nothing perfect.
Just real.

5
Final Chapter The Question

So here I am…
not trying to make things complicated again.

Just asking something simple.
Not expecting a perfect answer.
Not expecting it immediately.

But honestly.

👉 Will you be a part of this story?
